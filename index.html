Top visited safaris in Africa

Combining East Africa’s most significant wildlife experiences on one itinerary, this tour opens with gorilla trekking in Uganda’s teeming rainforests before flying you to the savannahs of the Masai Mara in Kenya and the Serengeti in Tanzania to marvel at the Great Wildebeest Migration.

The Zambezi is a natural playground with sunset boat cruises, thrilling white-water rafting and microlighting all on offer. From there, you cross the border to Kasane in Botswana to catch a light aircraft flight to the Moremi Game Reserve on the Okavango Delta.

Botswana is a premier wilderness destination, famous for its vast natural wildlife sanctuaries, including the Okavango Delta and Savuti Channel. These water havens stretch far along the grassy plains of Botswana and attract guests seeking to spot the world’s most endangered large mammals that come to drink at its life-giving waters.
